## 2. What this policy covers
This policy covers only the ZAYA **public website** (zaya.app). The ZAYA mobile
apps and platform, when available, will have their own separate privacy terms.

## 3. Information we collect
- **Information you give us.** When you use a form on this site (for example,
  "Join the pilot" or "Contact us"), we collect the details you provide: your
  **name** and **phone number**, and — if you provide them — your **email
  address**, the **role** you select (merchant, customer, rider, diaspora,
  partner), and any **message** you write.
- **Technical information.** Our hosting provider may automatically record
  limited technical data (such as your device's IP address and browser type)
  for security and to keep the site running. We keep this to a minimum.
- **Cookies.** This site uses minimal cookies. Any content-management login
  (for our own staff) uses functional cookies needed for that login. We do not
  use advertising cookies.

## 4. Why we use your information
We use the information you give us to: respond to your enquiry; contact you
about the ZAYA pilot and keep you informed if you asked us to; and understand
interest in ZAYA so we can plan the pilot. Our basis for processing is your
**consent** (you chose to contact us) and our legitimate interest in operating
ZAYA. You can withdraw consent at any time (see Section 8).

## 5. How we share information
We do **not** sell your personal information. We share it only: with the
service providers that operate this website and its forms on our behalf (our
website host and form service), who process it only as instructed by us; and
where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

## 9. Security
We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your
information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we

## 10. Children
This website is intended for adults (business owners, customers, partners). It
