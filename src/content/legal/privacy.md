# ZAYA Website — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** _set at go-live_ · **Last updated:** 12 July 2026

## 1. Who we are
This website (zayaethiopia.com) is operated by **ZAYA App PLC** ("ZAYA", "we", "us"),
Addis Ababa, Ethiopia (company registration in progress). This Privacy Policy
explains how we handle personal information you provide through this website.
Contact: **zayaapp@gmail.com · +251 91 283 5922 · +251 96 519 6475**.

## 2. What this policy covers
This policy covers only the ZAYA **public website** (zayaethiopia.com). The ZAYA mobile
apps and platform, when available, will have their own separate privacy terms.

## 3. Information we collect
- **Information you give us.** When you use a form on this site (for example,
  "Join the pilot" or "Contact us"), we collect the details you provide: your
  **name** and **phone number**, and — if you provide them — your **email
  address**, the **role** you select (merchant, customer, rider, diaspora,
  partner), and any **message** you write.
- **Technical information.** Our hosting provider may automatically record
  limited technical data (such as your device's IP address and browser type)
  for security and to keep the site running. We keep this to a minimum.
- **Cookies.** This site uses minimal cookies. Any content-management login
  (for our own staff) uses functional cookies needed for that login. We do not
  use advertising cookies.

## 4. Why we use your information
We use the information you give us to: respond to your enquiry; contact you
about the ZAYA pilot and keep you informed if you asked us to; and understand
interest in ZAYA so we can plan the pilot. Our basis for processing is your
**consent** (you chose to contact us) and our legitimate interest in operating
ZAYA. You can withdraw consent at any time (see Section 8).

## 5. How we share information
We do **not** sell your personal information. We share it only: with the
service providers that operate this website and its forms on our behalf (our
website host and form service), who process it only as instructed by us; and
where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

## 5a. The website assistant (AI chat)
This site offers a small **assistant chat**. Most answers come from ZAYA's own
published website content. For some English questions, the **text you type may
be sent to Google (Gemini)** — a third-party AI provider whose servers are
outside Ethiopia — to generate an answer. Before anything is sent, we
automatically remove email addresses and phone-number patterns, and we attach
**no account, no name, and no identity** to your message. Even so, **please do
not include personal details in the chat** — anything you type may be processed
by Google under its own terms. The assistant never handles orders, payments or
accounts, and if you want to reach a person, use the contact form (Section 3),
which does **not** go through any AI provider.

## 6. Where your information is stored
Our website and form service are provided by reputable third-party providers
whose servers may be located outside Ethiopia. Where your information is
transferred outside Ethiopia, we do so only where the provider is bound by
terms that protect it, and — for information you submit through our forms — on
the basis of your consent.

## 7. How long we keep it
We keep the contact details you submit for up to 24 months from your most
recent contact with us about the pilot, after which we delete them. If you
become a ZAYA user, the ZAYA app's own terms govern your account data.

## 8. Your rights
You have the right to: ask what personal information we hold about you; ask us
to correct information that is wrong; ask us to delete your information; and
withdraw your consent to be contacted. To exercise any of these, contact us at
zayaapp@gmail.com or the phone numbers above. We will respond to any such
request within 30 days.

## 9. Security
We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your
information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we
work to protect your data and to notify you and the relevant authority of any
serious breach as required by law.

## 10. Children
This website is intended for adults (business owners, customers, partners). It
is not directed at children, and you should not submit a child's personal
information through it.

## 11. Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version
here with a new "last updated" date.
